Dead Souls 2
Comments: Easy to use, polished, and popular.
Advantages: Works very well right out of the box, thoroughly documented.
Disadvantages: Uses the complex verb system. Function names are different from older libs.
Download/Homepage: http://dead-souls.net


Lima
Comments: Easy to use, polished, and popular.
Advantages: Works very well right out of the box, thoroughly documented.
Disadvantages: Not currently maintained. Not compatible with add_actions.
Download/Homepage: http://mudos.org/lima-1.0b5+driver.tar.gz


LPUniversity Mudlib
Comments: Not ready for play. Currently under development.
Advantages: Provides a chance to build a lib from the ground up without having to deal much with the driver.
Disadvantages: Currently it is in very early stages of development. Fixer-upper.
Download/Homepage: https://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=150383


Discworld
Comments: Well liked, but rarely used.
Advantages: The grotesquely popular Discworld MUD uses a variation of this lib, so it must have something going for it.
Disadvantages: Not a casual install. Some previous LP expertise and/or a lot of patience may be required to set up.
Download/Homepage: http://discworld.atuin.net/lpc/about/mudlib.html also http://lpmuds.net/files/discworld/
